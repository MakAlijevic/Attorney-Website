About Me

My Experience

She worked as a Legal Counsel in major factoring company Prvi Faktor on various complex legal aspects of numerous corporate, finance and regulatory matters; assisting with regard to due diligence investigations of individual financial aspects of the transactions, drafting and negotiating various transaction documents, as well as monitoring of implementation of all condition precedents and securitization of transactions including collection of bad debts. Before joining Prvi Faktor Mia was legal associate with B&H Prosecutors Office and was involved in several major investigations related to white-collar crime. Upon graduation Mia was legal advisor within the Anti-crime and corruption Department of the Office of High Representative, an institution established in line with the Dayton Peace Agreement implementing various legal and political aspects of the Dayton Peace Agreement including establishing of independent judiciary system in B&H.
